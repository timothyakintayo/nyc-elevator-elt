## Elevator Specific Insights for year 2024 and other service request analysis

Based on analysis of 22,507 elevator complaints in 2024, this report provides a descriptive analytics of elevator complaints and identifies critical market opportunities for a building maintenance company targeting NYC's elevator service market. 

## Total elevator complaint in year 2024
Total elevator complaints in year 2024: 22507

## Average number of days between complaint creation and closure of complaint
Average number of days to close elevator complaints: 51.5 days ~ 52 days

## Borough with highest elevator complaints
Bronx had the highest elevator complaints with 9340 complaints accounting for (41.5%) of the complaints

## Average time taken for lodged complaint to be updated after creation
It takes an average of 2.3 hours from the time when an complaint is created to when it gets updated.

## Month with highest complaints in year 2024
July had the highest complaints in the year 2024 with 2431 complaints accounting for 10.8% of the elevator complaints.

## Quarterly elevator complaint trend 2024
The third quarter had the highest elevator complaint in the year 2024 with 6514 complaints accounting for 28.94% of the reported elevator complaints.

## Geographic Concentration & HQ Location
The geographic analysis identified a hotspot at coordinates 40.822139, -73.940240 (Upper Manhattan/Bronx border area) with 57 complaints in a single geographic bin as the highest concentration detected.
This hotspot is significant because high complaint density implies high service demand. It is recommended that the headquarter is established within 3-mile radius of this hotspot (40.822139, -73.940240) to minimize emergency response times. The link to the hotspot can be found on [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps?q=40.822139,-73.940240)

## Other service request opportunities
Analysis of the overall complaints from year 2010 to 2023 showed the following top complaint types:
- HEAT/HOT WATER: 19,961 complaints (2022)
- PLUMBING: 4,398 complaints (2022)
- DOOR/WINDOW: 3,021 complaints (2022)
- ELECTRIC: 1,660 complaints (2022)


## Recommendation
Asides elevator service request opportunity, the business can target combining elevator request with HVAC/plumbing service presenting opportunity for increased revenue. Hence, technicians should be trained to identify and upsell secondary issues when attending to elevator request complaints such as heat/hot water, plumbing, door/window hardware, and electrical panel upgrades.
